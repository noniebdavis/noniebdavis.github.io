A modern hippie filling the world with a little more love one bead and gemstone at a time.


My name is Noël; I live in Southern California with my fiancé and our three rescue pups. I'm an advid animal lover, crafter, gardener, and an amateur cook. My friends like to call me the new Martha Stewart.


For the past fifteen years I have been creating loom jewelry and other hand crafted items. I freelance in many hobbies, but I truly love turning little beads into unique pieces of jewelry.


My love of beads inspired me to put together this website for other crafters in the hopes that it would inspire some, and offer help to others. I hope you enjoy my website's bead loom patterns, jewelry, and DIY projects.
